How to swim

Swimming has many purposes, to keep fit and it can be enjoyable.
It is a very important skil to have in life and death situations.

Swimming steps:
    -Let go of your fear.
    -Get used to foating.
    -Don't panic.
    -Practise exhaling underwater.
    -Practise kicking underwater.
    -Support your weight.
